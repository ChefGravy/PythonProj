About:

Hi, I am Ruslan. I am a real estate broker with North Pacific Properties.

North Pacific Properties is a privately owned real estate firm awarded 29th of the most successful companies in Puget Sound. North Pacific Properties is a full service real estate brokerage company located in Seattle, Washington. Our central mission is to provide our clients with expert and well-rounded real estate service throughout the Seattle and surrounding King County, Kitsap County, and Snohomish County areas while meeting the individual needs of our clients, one client at a time.

I grew up in Seattle and I love all the Pacific Northwest has to offer. Some of my favorite neighborhoods include Queen Anne, Capitol Hill, Ballard, Sand Point, Shoreline, and Edmonds. I hold a B.A. in Economics from UW and a Masters of Science in Finance from Seattle University. My goal is to make real estate as enjoyable and stress free as possible through my experience in real estate and my knowledge in finance.

In addition to real estate, I also enjoy developing Web Applications in JavaScript and Python. I created this website as a fun side project using Django 3.0.3 and Bootstrap V4.5. I will continue to improve and add features to this website over time so if there is something else you would like to see, please let me know.
